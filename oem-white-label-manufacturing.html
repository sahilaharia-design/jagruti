OEM + White Label + Export

OEM, Private-Label Manufacturing
& Global Export

Jagruti supports cookware brands, exporters, and private-label buyers that need components manufactured to their own product standards — not a standard catalogue. This page covers our OEM/white-label capabilities, the manufacturing clusters and export markets we serve, and the documentation international buyers need to import from us.

OEM & White-Label Capabilities

We supply the invisible-but-critical parts that fit inside your final product — manufactured to your spec, under your brand.

01
Custom Size & Diameter
Manufactured as per vessel format, cookware application, or buyer drawing. No catalogue constraint — every dimension is negotiable.
02
Thickness & Material Flexibility
SS 430, SS 304, mild steel, and application-specific grade selection. Thickness matched to functional and structural requirements.
03
Pattern & Perforation Development
Custom punching, perforation, grooving, and pattern development for cookware bottoms, steam plates, and precision components.
04
Embossing & Branding
Logo, pattern, identification, or functional embossing based on tooling feasibility. Your mark, our manufacturing.
05
Tooling & Die Development
In-house dies, moulds, samples, iterations, and approved production. No third-party toolmaker. Faster cycles, lower cost, your design stays private.
06
Repeat OEM Production
Built for monthly supply cycles, batch consistency, quality checks, and long-term vendor relationships. Mumbai base and Gujarat unit support scaling requirements.
07
Private-Label Confidentiality
Your specification, drawing and tooling stay confidential to your programme. Our name does not appear on your finished product.
08
Multi-SKU Programme Management
We manage multiple diameters, patterns and finishes under a single buyer relationship — one point of contact across your full component range.

How OEM & Private-Label Sourcing Works

Four steps, in-house tooling throughout, no third-party toolmaker in the loop.

1
You Share the Spec
Drawing, physical sample, or a written dimension. We work from whatever you have.
2
We Build the Tooling
Die and tool development entirely in-house. Faster, cheaper, your IP stays on our floor.
3
Sample & Approval
Prototype batch produced. You inspect, test, approve. No bulk run until you sign off.
4
Bulk Production & Delivery
High-volume output, multi-stage quality checks, delivered domestically or exported per your documentation needs.

Export Documentation & Compliance

What international buyers need for customs clearance, GST and letter-of-credit documentation.

HSN Codes for Export & Import Documentation
Customs · GST · Shipping · LC Documentation
7220 9090
Circle-cut Stainless Steel Blanks
Flat-rolled SS blanks, cut to circular form. Covers induction plate blanks and stainless steel discs before finishing. Used for GST invoicing of raw blanks and export documentation.
7326 9099
Finished & Semi-finished Induction Base Plates
Stamped, embossed or formed articles of iron or steel. Covers finished induction base plates and processed cookware components. Standard HSN for export invoicing and LC documentation.
